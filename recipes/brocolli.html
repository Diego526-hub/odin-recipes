Roasted broccoli made in the air fryer and served with a savory queso fresco cheese sauce makes a quick and easy side dish.

Ingridients

  • 6 cups broccoli florets
  • 10 tablespoons low-fat evaporated milk
  • 5 tablespoons queso fresco, crumbled
  • 6 lower-sodium saltine crackers

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Coat broccoli florets with cooking spray. Put half of the florets in thefryer basket. Cook until crisp-tender, 6 to 8 minutes. Repeat with remaining broccoli.
  3. Meanwhile, add evaporated milk, queso fresco, aji amarillo paste, and saltines to a blender; process until smooth, about 45 seconds. Pour sauce into a bowl. Microwave until warm, about 30 seconds. Serve with broccoli.
